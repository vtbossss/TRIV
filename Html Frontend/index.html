About Us

A Gateway to Precision Agriculture
"T.R.I.V."

TRIV integrates advanced real-time data visualization and analytics to revolutionize decision-making in precision agriculture.

The platform combines weather forecasts, NDVI history, and agro data to support efficient resource allocation and crop health monitoring.

Designed to enhance productivity, TRIV offers a seamless interface for farmers and researchers, leveraging modern technologies like WebSockets, Three.js, and Kafka.
